Welcome To Our 11'th



The term "11th hour"

refers to a time of great urgency or a deadline that is rapidly approaching. It can also refer to a situation where a decision or action must be taken at the very last moment before it is too late. The phrase is often used in the context of making a last-ditch effort to achieve a goal or avoid a negative outcome. The origin of the phrase is believed to come from the Bible, specifically from the parable of the workers in the vineyard, in which the owner of the vineyard hires workers throughout the day, with the last group being hired at the 11th hour.
